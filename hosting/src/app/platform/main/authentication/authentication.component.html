Frequently asked questions

  • I cannot login?

    You may have already registered with a social media account, try using the options available.

  • When I login my account details are missing

    You may have already registered with another email address.

  • Error message: There is no user record corresponding to this identifier. The user may have been deleted.

    There is no active account for the email address given, this may be because a different email address was used or an account was never created.

  • Error message: The password is invalid or the user does not have a password.

    This may be due to having created your account using a social media platform.
